This journal records the phylogenetic observations made within the Gorge known as the Throat — a vertical descent of some seventeen zones through which all known life in this subterranean system has been documented, classified, and where possible, named.

Entries are organised according to the D&D creature-type convention as adapted for the deep: Domains, Kingdoms, Phyla, Classes, Orders, Families, Genera, and Species. Common ancestors for which no specimen has been recovered are noted as theorised, and marked ? thus. Extinct or fossil-grade taxa are marked † thus. Organisms not native to the Gorge but included for taxonomic context are noted accordingly.
